### Page Authority Flow

```
Homepage (DA anchor)
    │
    ├──→ High PR internal links to all Tier 2 service pages
    ├──→ High PR internal links to 2 primary area pages
    │         (Karaikal + Mayiladuthurai)
    │
Service Pages ──→ Cross-link to related services
    │         ──→ Link to relevant area pages
    │         ──→ Link to blog articles on same topic
    │
Area Pages ──→ Link to all relevant services for that city
    │      ──→ Link to projects in that city
    │
Blog Posts ──→ Support both service and area pages with backlinks
           ──→ Always include 2–3 contextual links upward
```

**Nav Behaviour Rules:**
- Default state: `background: transparent` — hero image shows through
- Scrolled state (>80px): `background: rgba(10,22,40,0.96); backdrop-filter: blur(20px)`
- Active page: gold underline, 2px, animated slide-in
- "Get Quote" button: always gold, no transparency
- Dropdown: appears on hover (desktop) with 200ms delay (prevents accidental trigger)
- Dropdown animation: fade + translateY(8px→0)

## 04 · DESIGN SYSTEM

### 4.1 Design Principles

| Principle | Application |
|---|---|
| **Confident Restraint** | Never use 3 elements where 1 works. White space is active, not empty. |
| **Typography First** | Headlines carry 70% of the visual weight. Photography supports, not leads. |
| **Gold as Signal** | Gold (#B8892A) is used ONLY for interactive elements, highlights, and trust moments — never decoratively. |
| **Mobile-First Composition** | Every layout decision is first solved at 375px, then expanded outward. |
| **Motion with Purpose** | Animations reveal meaning — they don't decorate. Every animation has a semantic reason. |
| **Accessibility as Default** | WCAG 2.1 AA is the floor, not a stretch goal. |

**Why Cormorant Garamond:**  
Used by Renzo Piano Building Workshop, Vitra Design Museum, Zaha Hadid Architects. It carries the visual DNA of architecture — structural, elegant, precise. At large sizes it looks like a building drawing. It's free on Google Fonts. For a construction company aiming at premium positioning, this single font choice separates KTR from every other local builder who uses Poppins or Roboto.
