Construction Process

How KTR Builders Constructs a House in Mayiladuthurai

Step 01

Site Visit & Soil Assessment

Er. K. Sasi Kumar visits the plot. Soil condition and bearing capacity assessment. Foundation type determination. Plot boundary verification against patta documents. Building regulation requirements for the plot's location in Mayiladuthurai district.

Step 02

Structural Design & Drawings

Complete structural drawings prepared by Er. Sasi Kumar: foundation plan, column and beam layout, slab design, staircase design. All drawings are engineer-signed. Architectural drawings (floor plan, elevation, section) coordinated with structural.

Step 03

Building Plan Approval

Building plan submitted to the relevant planning authority for Mayiladuthurai district (Tamil Nadu regulations — not KPA, which applies to Karaikal only). Construction does not commence without the approved building plan.

Step 04

Foundation & Plinth

Foundation excavation, PCC, reinforcement placement, concrete pour. Er. Sasi Kumar present for reinforcement inspection and concrete pour. Plinth beam and plinth filling completed before superstructure begins.

Step 05

Superstructure

Column casting, lintel, roof slab — each stage inspected and approved by Er. Sasi Kumar before the next stage begins. Concrete quality verified at pour. No structural stage proceeds without engineer sign-off.

Step 06

Finishing & Handover

Plastering, electrical, plumbing, flooring, painting, joinery. Final walkthrough. Structural sign-off document issued. Handover to client.
