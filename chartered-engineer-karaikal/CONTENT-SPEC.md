---

**Q1: What is a Chartered Engineer and how is it different from a regular engineer?**

A Chartered Engineer is formally recognised by the Institution of Engineers India, requiring an accredited engineering degree, proven professional experience, a competency assessment, and commitment to continuing professional development. The credential is verifiable through the IEI registry at ieindia.org. It is not a self-declared title, a marketing term, or a company description. Er. K. Sasi Kumar holds Chartered Engineer status from the IEI.

---

**Q2: What does M.I.E. mean — and what does it mean for my project?**

M.I.E. stands for Member of the Institution of Engineers India — India's oldest engineering professional body, founded in 1920. Membership requires a recognised engineering qualification and professional standing, and is bound by the IEI code of professional conduct. For your project: the engineer supervising your work is governed by national professional standards. If he acts negligently, professional accountability extends beyond the company.

---

**Q3: What does F.I.V. mean — can Er. Sasi Kumar do property valuations?**

F.I.V. stands for Fellow of the Institution of Valuers — a formal property valuation qualification. As an F.I.V., Er. K. Sasi Kumar is qualified to conduct certified property valuations required for bank loan documentation, legal proceedings, and asset assessment. This is relevant when you are building and simultaneously seeking construction finance — the same professional can handle both the construction supervision and the property valuation.

---

**Q4: What does KPA Registered Engineer mean for my building plan?**

KPA Registered Engineer means Er. K. Sasi Kumar is individually registered with the Karaikal Planning Authority as a qualified professional authorised to prepare and submit building plans. Building plans submitted without a KPA Registered Engineer's signature are not accepted by the KPA. Er. Sasi Kumar has been KPA Registered since 2005 and has had over 250 building plans approved. Note: KPA applies to Karaikal district only.

---

**Q5: How do I verify Er. K. Sasi Kumar's credentials independently?**

Each credential has a verifying body:
- Chartered Engineer and M.I.E. — Institution of Engineers India: ieindia.org
- F.I.V. — Institution of Valuers
- KPA Registration — Karaikal Planning Authority

These are not self-declared titles. They are credentials issued by formal professional bodies and maintained through those bodies. Independent verification is encouraged.

---

**Q6: Is Er. K. Sasi Kumar available for structural assessments of existing buildings?**

Yes. Er. Sasi Kumar conducts structural assessments of existing buildings — for renovation feasibility, floor addition assessment, and structural safety evaluation. With 28+ years of Karaikal district construction experience, he has assessed structures of all ages and types across the coastal region. Contact him at +91 90470 26074 to discuss your specific requirement.
