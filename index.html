01

How much does house construction cost in Karaikal?

Construction cost depends on the design, floor area, and material grade specified. We provide a written estimate after a site visit — not a guessed figure over the phone. The estimate covers structure, materials, and engineering fees with no hidden additions.

↳ Written estimate provided after site assessment · No hidden costs

02

How long does construction take from start to handover?

A standard G+0 residential build typically takes 8–12 months from approved plan to handover. G+1 builds are typically 12–18 months. We provide a project timeline at the planning stage — agreed in writing before construction begins.

↳ Timeline agreed in writing at project start

03

How does KPA plan approval work, and how long does it take?

The Karaikal Planning Authority requires structural drawings prepared by a registered engineer. Once submitted, approval typically takes 30–60 days. With 250+ plans approved, KTR Builders prepares drawings that meet KPA requirements at first submission — avoiding delays from rejection and resubmission.

↳ 250+ KPA-approved plans · First-submission track record

04

Is Er. Sasi Kumar personally involved in every project?

Yes. Er. K. Sasi Kumar, B.E., M.I.E., F.I.V., personally supervises every project — from structural drawings to site inspection to final handover. KTR Builders does not delegate supervision to a junior team. If you build with KTR, you build with Er. Sasi Kumar.

↳ Er. K. Sasi Kumar · Founder · Present on every project

05

Do you build in Mayiladuthurai as well as Karaikal?

Yes. KTR Builders takes on residential and commercial projects across Karaikal and Mayiladuthurai districts. KPA approvals and structural design are handled for both areas. Contact us to discuss your site location and project requirements.

↳ Active projects in Karaikal & Mayiladuthurai
